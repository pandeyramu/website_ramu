About Me

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Ramu Pandey, a student from Nepal who is learning web development one project at a time. My main focus is HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I enjoy turning simple ideas into interactive pages that feel useful and practical. I started this site to document my progress, share what I learn, and build a body of work that shows how I grow through practice instead of waiting for perfect knowledge.

I built these projects because I believe learning becomes stronger when it is connected to real things. A calculator teaches logic, a game teaches state management, a typing test teaches timing and accuracy, and a blog teaches content structure and communication. Each project helps me understand how design, code, and user experience fit together.

My goal is to become a better frontend developer and eventually work on larger, more meaningful projects for students and users in Nepal. I want to keep improving my skills, share clear explanations, and create web tools that are both simple and genuinely helpful.
