Disclaimer

Last updated: May 18, 2026

CEE MCQ is an educational practice website built to support students preparing for Nepal's Common Entrance Examination. Please read this disclaimer carefully before relying on any content from this site.

Educational Purpose Only

The questions, explanations, blog posts, and revision materials on CEE MCQ are provided to help students practice and revise. They are not an official exam paper, a government publication, or a guaranteed answer key. CEE MCQ is not affiliated with the Medical Education Commission or any official body that conducts the Common Entrance Examination.

Accuracy and Simplification

We try to keep content accurate and useful, but some topics may be simplified for faster revision. This means a concept may be explained in a way that supports quick recall, even where the official syllabus, textbook, or exam wording is more detailed or precise. Students should cross-check important facts, especially borderline or high-stakes concepts, with trusted textbooks, class notes, and official exam sources.

No Guarantee of Results

Practicing on CEE MCQ does not guarantee any particular score, rank, or outcome in the actual examination. Performance depends on many factors beyond the scope of this site, including overall study effort, official syllabus coverage, and exam-day conditions. CEE MCQ should be treated as one part of a broader preparation strategy, not a substitute for complete syllabus study.

Limitation of Liability

We are not responsible for any loss, missed marks, or other consequences resulting from reliance on this site's content. Users are encouraged to treat CEE MCQ as a practice and revision tool rather than a final authority on exam content or outcomes.

Reporting Errors

If you notice an incorrect question, an unclear explanation, or any other mistake, please let us know through the Contact page or the in-app report option on a question. We review reported issues and use them to improve the question bank and content over time.
