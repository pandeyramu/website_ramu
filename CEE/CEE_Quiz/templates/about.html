About Us

CEE MCQ is a free practice platform built for Nepali students who are preparing for the Common Entrance Examination. The goal of the site is simple: make revision easier, faster, and more structured by giving learners chapter-wise MCQs, topic-based practice, and full-test simulations in one place. Instead of scrolling through scattered notes or relying only on memorization, students can use the site to test what they know and identify where they need more revision.

This project was created by Ramu Pandey with a practical focus on real exam preparation. The platform is designed to support students in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and MAT by offering short practice sessions that can fit into a busy study schedule. Each quiz is intended to help students learn through repetition, recall, and timing, which are important for competitive exams.

CEE MCQ is also built to be useful beyond question practice. It includes preparation tips, blog articles, and helpful pages that explain how to study more effectively. The aim is to create a student-friendly educational site that feels clear, useful, and focused on exam results. Over time, more original study resources and revision guides can be added to make the platform even more valuable for Nepali students.
