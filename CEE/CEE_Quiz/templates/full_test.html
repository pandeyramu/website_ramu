What is the CEE Full Mock Test?

This full-length mock test simulates the actual Common Entrance Examination (CEE) conducted by the Medical Education Commission (MEC) in Nepal. It contains 180 questions covering Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, and MAT — exactly matching the real exam blueprint.

Each question carries +1 mark for a correct answer and −0.25 marks for a wrong answer. You have 2.5 hours to complete the test. The timer starts as soon as you begin, and your score, accuracy, and per-question speed are calculated automatically after submission.

Practicing the full test regularly helps you build exam stamina, identify weak areas, and improve time management before your actual CEE.
