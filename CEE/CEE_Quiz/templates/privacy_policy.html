Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

CEE MCQ is designed to be a lightweight educational website. We do not aim to collect large amounts of personal data. In normal browsing, most visitors can use the site without creating an account. If you submit a contact form, we may receive the name, email address, and message you enter so we can respond to your request.

When you attempt MCQs or full tests, the site may store minimal quiz-related information such as your name, topic, score, and timing details. This helps display results, keep session progress, and make the learning experience smoother. We do not sell personal data, and we do not use collected information for anything unrelated to education, support, or basic site operation.

The site may use cookies and browser storage for login-free session support, quiz progress, and preferences. Third-party services such as Google AdSense or analytics tools may also use cookies or similar identifiers to deliver ads, measure traffic, and understand usage patterns. These services operate under their own policies, and users can manage cookie settings from their browser or Google ad settings where available.

We take reasonable steps to protect site data, but no online system is completely risk-free. If you want to request data correction or deletion, or if you have a privacy question, you can contact us through the Contact page or email support directly. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy and any future updates published on this page.
