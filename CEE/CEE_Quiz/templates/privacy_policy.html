Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

CEE MCQ is a lightweight educational website built for students preparing for Nepal's Common Entrance Examination. This policy explains what information the site collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you. By using CEE MCQ, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

Most visitors can browse chapters, attempt MCQs, and use the site without creating an account. We collect information only in two situations:

  • Contact form submissions: if you reach out through the Contact page, we receive the name, email address, and message you provide so we can respond to your query.
  • Quiz and test attempts: when you take an MCQ or full mock test, the site stores minimal quiz-related details such as your name, the topic attempted, your score, and timing information. This is used to display your results, support your test history, and keep your progress consistent during a session.

We do not require account creation, and we do not collect sensitive personal information such as payment details, government identification, or location data.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through this site is used only for educational, support, or basic site-operation purposes, such as responding to contact messages, displaying your quiz results, and improving the accuracy of the question bank over time. We do not sell personal data to third parties, and we do not use the information you provide for marketing unrelated to CEE MCQ.

Cookies and Third-Party Services

CEE MCQ may use cookies and browser storage to support login-free quiz sessions, remember your progress, and store basic preferences. In addition, third-party services such as Google AdSense and analytics tools may use cookies, pixel tags, or similar technologies to serve relevant ads, measure site traffic, and understand how the site is used.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies, which we do not control. You can review and manage how Google uses data for ad personalization through Google Ads Settings, and you can disable cookies generally through your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

CEE MCQ is intended for students preparing for a secondary-level entrance examination and does not knowingly collect personal information from young children beyond what is voluntarily entered in the name field for quiz attempts or the contact form. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through this site and you would like it removed, please contact us using the details below.

Links to Other Websites

This site may contain links to external resources, including past-paper sources or reference material. CEE MCQ is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites, and we encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit through a link on CEE MCQ.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical steps to protect the data collected through this site. However, no online platform can guarantee complete security, and we cannot promise the absolute security of any information transmitted to or through CEE MCQ.

Your Choices

You may request correction or deletion of personal information you have submitted, such as a contact form entry, by reaching out through the Contact page. We will respond to reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the site, applicable law, or third-party service requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of CEE MCQ after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please reach out through the Contact page.
