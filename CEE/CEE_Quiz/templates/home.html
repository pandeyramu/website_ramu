The Common Entrance Examination (CEE) is conducted by Nepal's Medical Education Commission (MEC) for admission into health science programs like MBBS, BDS, and B.Sc. Nursing across Nepal's medical colleges. It is held once a year, typically between July and September.

The exam consists of 200 MCQ questions to be completed in 3 hours, covering Physics (50), Chemistry (50), Biology {Zoology + Botany} (80), and a Mental Agility Test (20). Each correct answer carries 1 mark, while an incorrect answer deducts 0.25 marks, making the maximum possible score 200.
